The situation with energy carriers

Sharp increase in mineral extraction

Over the past 80 years, more minerals have been extracted from the Earth's interior than in the entire history of mankind.

Remains of energy resources

According to average estimates, in the explored fields of the world oil will last for 15-25 years, gas - for 30-50 years, coal - for 100-200 years, at the current rate of production and consumption.

The necessary steps

To ensure sustainable development, it is necessary to reduce oil consumption by 85%, natural gas - by 70%, coal - by 20%. All mineral resources should be considered as exhaustible and non-renewable.
